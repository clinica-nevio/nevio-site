Cases

Text-only examples — no photos, focused on process and outcomes.

1) Sensitivity and inflamed gums

Problem: bleeding when brushing, cold sensitivity, tartar build-up.

Treatment: full hygiene visit + at-home routine + follow-up check.

Outcome: less discomfort, healthier gums, simple maintenance plan.

2) Pain when chewing (deep decay)

Problem: biting pain and persistent sensitivity.

Treatment: root canal therapy + restoration for protection.

Outcome: pain resolved, tooth preserved and stable.

3) Uneven front teeth and staining

Problem: uneven appearance, stains that don’t improve with brushing.

Treatment: hygiene + whitening (when indicated) + selective aesthetic restorations.

Outcome: more uniform smile with a natural look and easy maintenance.
